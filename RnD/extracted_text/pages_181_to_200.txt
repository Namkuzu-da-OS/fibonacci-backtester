TIME FRAME'S IMPACT ON FOCUS NUMBER:
If we then lengthened the Time Frame significantly, as in Chart 10-10, the resulting
Market Swings would show up as much simpler waves. Think about it; if you go from a
five minute to an hourly chart, there will be fewer discernible retracements, i.e. Reaction
Points will disappear. It's the same situation any time you increase your Time Frame.
Likewise by reducing your Time Frame, you will get more Reaction Points, more
Confluence areas, and more opportunity to fine tune your entry and profit objectives.
